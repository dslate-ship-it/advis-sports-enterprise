Founder & Managing Partner — The Advisory

Jimmie B. Strong founded The Advisory on a singular conviction: that the athletes, entertainers, and entrepreneurs who create the world's most exceptional personal value deserve the same institutional-grade enterprise management that global corporations take for granted.

His path to founding The Advisory is as rare as the clients he serves. A Harvard Law School Valedictorian, Jimmie combined legal precision with an equally rigorous background in financial advisory — ultimately earning recognition as a Top 1% Financial Advisor nationally while simultaneously building his practice in sports and entertainment law.

At Vanderbilt University, where he serves on the faculty, Jimmie teaches the intersection of business strategy and legal structure — the same framework that underpins every engagement at The Advisory. His clients have included Floyd Mayweather, Mookie Betts, Jalen Ramsey, Jaren Jackson Jr., and the late Jim Brown.

Jimmie's philosophy is simple and uncompromising: you should keep what you earn, own what you create, and understand your enterprise at every stage. The Advisory exists to make that possible.
