Privacy Policy Terms of Use Disclosures

This Privacy Policy describes how The Advisory (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our enterprise management website or submit an inquiry through contact.html. This site provides information about enterprise management services for professional athletes, NIL athletes, entertainers, creatives, and high-net-worth entrepreneurs. Legal services offered by The Advisory, PLLC are provided through a separate website at theadvisorypllc.com, which may have its own privacy practices.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Information you provide. When you use our contact form, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, inquiry category (for example, professional athlete, NIL student-athlete, entertainer, entrepreneur, agent, or advisor), sport or industry, primary need, and any message you choose to submit.
  • Technical information. Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically collect certain technical data when you visit, such as IP address, browser type, device type, referring URL, and pages viewed. This information is generally used for security, performance, and basic analytics.
  • Communications. If you email us or correspond with us after submitting an inquiry, we retain those communications as part of our intake and client relationship records.

How We Use Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule consultations;
  • Evaluate whether our enterprise management services may be appropriate for your situation;
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website;
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats; and
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not sell your personal information.

How We Share Information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers. Third parties that help us operate our website and intake process, such as form processing and email delivery providers, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Affiliated professionals. Where appropriate to respond to your inquiry, information may be shared with attorneys or other professionals associated with The Advisory, PLLC or our enterprise management practice.
  • Legal requirements. We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental request, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect rights, safety, or property.

Data Retention

We retain inquiry and contact information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request, evaluate a potential engagement, maintain business records, and comply with applicable law. If an attorney-client or advisory relationship is formed, separate retention policies may apply under engagement terms.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information submitted through our site. No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information. To make a request, contact us at admin@theadvisory.global. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. NIL-related inquiries involving student-athletes should be submitted by the athlete, parent, guardian, or authorized representative as appropriate.

Third-Party Links

Our site links to third-party websites, including The Advisory, PLLC law firm site and external professional resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above indicates when this policy was last revised. Material changes will be posted on this page.
