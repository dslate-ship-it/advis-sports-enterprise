About The Advisory

Built on trust.
Measured in legacies.

The Advisory exists because institutional-grade enterprise management should not be the exclusive province of the ultra-wealthy. Every athlete and creative who earns at the highest level deserves the systems that protect and grow what they've built.

We are not a management company. We are not a single-specialty law firm. We are the complete back office for the businesses of exceptional people.

Jimmie B. Strong · Founder
Our Founder

Jimmie B. Strong

Founder | Sports, Entertainment, Private Client and Family Office Advisory

Jimmie B. Strong advises athletes, entertainers, founders, investors, family offices and high net worth clients on the legal, business and financial architecture behind long term wealth.

His work sits at the intersection of sports law, entertainment, corporate transactions, investment strategy, family office design, brand monetization, tax aware planning and professional service provider management.

Representative Focus

NIL, Student Athlete and Parent Advisory

Jimmie advises high school athletes, college athletes and their families on the business and legal decisions that follow athletic visibility.

Areas of focus include:

  • NIL contract review and negotiation
  • Brand partnership structure
  • Sponsorship and endorsement agreements
  • Agent selection and management
  • Investor and advisor screening
  • Parent education and decision support
  • School, booster, collective and third party relationship review
  • Career planning beyond eligibility
  • Tax, entity and payment structure coordination
  • Long term wealth planning before professional income arrives

Professional Athlete and Entertainer Advisory

For professional athletes, entertainers and creators, Jimmie helps build the operating structure around the career, not just the contract.

Advisory areas include:

  • Representation strategy
  • Agent, manager, attorney and advisor coordination
  • Family office setup and management
  • Endorsement and licensing opportunities
  • Business entity formation
  • Investment review and transaction structure
  • Private equity and venture capital access
  • Brand ownership and monetization
  • Real estate, operating business and startup investment review
  • Tax optimization in coordination with tax professionals
  • Succession, estate and legacy planning coordination
  • Professional service provider oversight

Family Office and Private Client Infrastructure

Jimmie works with clients whose income, reputation, relationships and deal flow require more than traditional legal support.

His family office and private client work includes:

  • Building the client’s advisory team
  • Coordinating attorneys, accountants, agents, managers, bankers and investment professionals
  • Reviewing investment opportunities before capital is committed
  • Structuring operating companies, holding companies and affiliated entities
  • Designing governance around family, business and investment decisions
  • Managing conflicts between business partners, family members, investors and representatives
  • Supporting wealth preservation, growth and transfer planning
  • Creating systems that allow clients to evaluate opportunities without being overrun by them

Sports and Entertainment Experience

Jimmie’s sports and entertainment work includes advisory services for athletes, entertainers, college programs and student athletes.

Notable experience includes:

  • Earned extensive sports and entertainment law coursework while at Harvard Law School
  • Partner in ESQ Agency, a talent management agency representing athletes, writers, producers and creative artists
  • Provides curriculum development and career development services to college sports programs and student athletes

Corporate, Investment and Transactional Background

Jimmie’s private client work is supported by a corporate and investment background that includes securities law, mergers and acquisitions, private equity, venture capital, emerging companies and fund formation.

  • Former General Counsel of Rally Network, Inc., a creator economy platform focused on digital economies, cryptocurrencies and NFTs

  • Former associate in Baker Donelson’s securities, mergers and acquisitions and emerging companies practices

  • Provides counsel on entity formation, intellectual property protection, capital strategy, governance and strategic expansion

Jimmie’s work is built for clients whose careers, businesses and capital attract opportunity before they have the infrastructure to manage it.

  • How should I get paid

What We Believe

Six principles that guide every engagement.

01
You Keep What You Earn
Every engagement is designed to close the gap between what you earn and what you keep. Tax strategy, entity structure, and deal terms all serve this one principle.
02
Clarity Over Dependency
We build systems that give you clarity and control — not dependency on any single advisor. You should understand your enterprise at every stage.
03
No Conflicts. Ever.
We earn when you win. No commissions on investments. No undisclosed relationships. No interests that compete with yours — under any circumstances.
04
Long-Term Thinking
Careers end. Enterprises don't have to. We build structures that serve you for decades — not just for the next deal.
05
Institutional Standards for Everyone
The same rigor we bring to multi-billion dollar entities, applied to every client relationship — regardless of where they are in their career.
06
Selective by Design
We take a limited number of new client relationships each year. This is not exclusivity for its own sake — it is the only way to deliver what we promise.

Our client relationships are built over time, not over transactions. When a global icon trusts us with their enterprise, it reflects not just our capabilities — but our commitment to their long-term position.
