Enterprise Management and Legal Services

This website describes enterprise management services offered by The Advisory. Legal services are provided by The Advisory, PLLC through its separate website at theadvisorypllc.com. The two practices are related but distinct. Nothing on this Site should be understood as legal advice or as creating an attorney-client relationship with The Advisory, PLLC unless and until a formal engagement is established in writing.

General Information Only

Content on this Site—including descriptions of tax strategy, legal structuring, brand and IP ownership, family office and governance, NIL compliance, contracts, and deal diligence—is provided for general informational purposes. Laws, regulations, NCAA and conference rules, state NIL requirements, tax rules, and industry standards change frequently. Site content may not reflect the most current developments and may not apply to your jurisdiction or circumstances.

No Investment or Tax Advice

Unless otherwise expressly agreed in a written engagement, information on this Site does not constitute investment advice, securities recommendations, or tax advice. References to financial planning, wealth architecture, entity structure, or deal diligence are descriptive of service areas and do not, by themselves, establish an advisory or fiduciary relationship. Any investment, tax, or financial strategy should be evaluated with qualified professionals based on your specific facts.

No Guarantee of Results

Case descriptions, service summaries, statistics, rankings, credentials, and references to notable client relationships illustrate experience and capability. They are not promises or guarantees of outcomes in any future matter. Results depend on facts, law, market conditions, and client objectives.

Conflict-Free Advisory

The Advisory is structured to provide conflict-free enterprise management. We do not accept commissions on investments, maintain undisclosed referral arrangements, or take positions adverse to client interests in the ordinary course of our work. Specific conflict policies applicable to an engagement will be addressed in engagement documentation.

Partnerships and Referrals

Introducer, referral, or partnership arrangements are documented separately with transparent terms. Submission of a partnership inquiry does not create a binding agreement.

Inquiries and Confidentiality

We treat intake inquiries as confidential business communications. However, submitting information through the Site or by email does not create attorney-client privilege or a protected advisory relationship. Do not send sensitive documents or highly confidential information until an engagement and secure transmission method have been established. You may request a consultation through our contact form.
